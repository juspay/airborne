Welcome to the **Airborne blog** — a place for the things that don't belong in reference docs: what's new, how we think about safe over-the-air rollouts, and the occasional look under the hood.

## What you'll find here

- **Release notes** — what changed in the SDKs, CLIs, server, and dashboard, and what it means for your rollouts.
- **Engineering notes** — deep dives into how Airborne works, from the [download & boot flow](/docs/concepts/download-and-boot-flow) to targeting and cohorts.
- **Rollout tips** — practical guidance on shipping OTA updates safely: version cohorts, ramping traffic, checkpoints, and instant rollbacks.

## New to Airborne?

Airborne delivers over-the-air updates to your React Native, Android, and iOS apps — with precise targeting, gradual ramps, and instant rollbacks. If you're just getting started:

- Read the [Introduction](/docs/intro) for how the pieces fit together.
- Follow [Integrate in React Native](/docs/guides/integrate-react-native) to ship your first OTA release end to end.
- Browse the [API Reference](/docs/api-reference/overview) for the server contract.
