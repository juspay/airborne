An **organisation** is the top-level workspace in Airborne. It owns your [applications](/docs/dashboard/applications) and the [users](/docs/dashboard/users-and-roles) who can access them. You always work inside one organisation at a time, and you can belong to several.

<Screenshot name="org-overview" caption="The organisation overview lists every application and is where you create new ones." />

## Selecting and switching organisations

The current organisation is shown in the **organisation switcher** at the top-left of the header. Click it to:

- Switch to another organisation you belong to, or
- Choose **`+ Create Organisation`** to make a new one.

Selecting an organisation takes you to its overview.

## Creating an organisation

Choose **`+ Create Organisation`** from the switcher (or create your first one during [onboarding](/docs/dashboard/authentication-and-onboarding)). In the **Create Organisation** dialog, enter an **Organisation name** (up to 64 characters, for example `acme_corp`) and choose **Create**.

:::note
If your deployment restricts organisation creation, the option becomes **`+ Request Organisation`**. Fill in the form — organisation name, your name, email, and optional store links — and an administrator will provision the organisation for you.
:::

## The organisation overview

The overview is the home page for an organisation. It is titled **Organization Overview** and lets you:

- See every **application** in the organisation as a card. Use the **Search applications…** box to filter by name.
- Open an application with its **Open** button to jump into the [application overview](/docs/dashboard/applications).
- Create a new application with **`+ Create Application`** (see below).

At the organisation level the left sidebar shows just two items: **Overview** and **Users**. To manage who belongs to the organisation and what they can do, see [Users & roles](/docs/dashboard/users-and-roles).

## Creating an application from the organisation

Choose **`+ Create Application`** on the overview. In the **Create New Application** dialog enter:

- **Application name** (required, up to 64 characters), and
- an optional **Description**.

Choose **Create Application** and the new app appears as a card. See [Applications](/docs/dashboard/applications) for what to do next.

:::tip
Use a separate application per shippable target — for example one app per platform — since files, packages, releases, and tokens are all scoped to a single application.
:::
