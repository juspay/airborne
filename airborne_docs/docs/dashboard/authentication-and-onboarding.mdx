Before you can use the Airborne dashboard you sign in to your account. Depending on how your Airborne deployment is configured you can authenticate with a username and password, with a single-sign-on (OIDC) provider, or both. New deployments then walk you through creating your first organisation so you have somewhere to ship to.

## Signing in

Open the dashboard and you land on the **Sign in** screen.

<Screenshot name="auth-login" caption="The sign-in screen. Password fields and SSO buttons appear depending on how your deployment is configured." />

To sign in with a password, enter your **Username** and **Password** and choose **Sign in**. Tick **Remember me** to stay signed in on this device.

If your deployment enables single sign-on, you will also see one or more **`Continue with <provider>`** buttons (for example `Continue with Google`). Choosing one redirects you to your identity provider and back to the dashboard once you authenticate.

:::note
Which options appear is controlled by your Airborne server. Some deployments show only SSO, only password login, or both separated by an "Or continue with" divider.
:::

## Creating an account

If self-registration is enabled, the sign-in screen shows a **Sign up** link. The registration form collects your **First name**, **Last name**, **Email**, and a **Password** (entered twice to confirm), and asks you to accept the terms.

<Screenshot name="auth-register" caption="The registration form. You can also register through an SSO provider when configured." />

Choose **Create account** to finish. If registration is not available on your deployment, ask an administrator to invite you — see [Users & roles](/docs/dashboard/users-and-roles).

## First-run onboarding: your first organisation

Everything in Airborne lives inside an [organisation](/docs/dashboard/organisations), so the first thing a brand-new account does is create one. When you have no organisations yet, the dashboard shows a **Create your first Organisation** card.

<Screenshot name="onboarding-create-first-org" caption="On first run you are prompted to create your first organisation before anything else." />

Enter an **Organisation name** (up to 64 characters) and choose **Create Organisation**. You are taken straight into the new organisation, where you can [create your first application](/docs/dashboard/applications).

:::note
Some deployments restrict who can create organisations. If creation is disabled for your account, the card becomes **Request your first Organisation** instead — fill in the requested details and an administrator will provision one for you.
:::
